[field recordings]

my field recording practice is concerned with the acoustic ecology of remote and transitional landscapes — the sounds that exist just beyond the built world. some of this work has appeared in print, broadcast, and installation contexts, including an extended collaboration with The Narwhal on the subject of protecting natural soundscapes.

[about]

jonathan kawchuk is a canadian composer and sound artist whose practice spans installation, field recording, and original scores for documentary and narrative film.

his work draws on the textures of remote landscapes — the boreal, the arctic, the coastal inlet — treated as acoustic sites rather than backdrops. he has made recordings across canada for broadcast, installation, and print contexts.

on screen, recent collaborations include work for national geographic and other documentary contexts, often alongside composer walker grimshaw.
