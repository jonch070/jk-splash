jonathan kawchuk

+ about

The natural world is perfect. I go to great lengths to reflect that in my work.

I make sound pieces, field recordings, and film scores. My work has taken me from the Canadian Arctic to the Phi Centre in Montreal, from National Geographic documentaries to quiet parks advocacy.

I believe in the radical importance of silence — real silence, the kind found in wilderness. I co-founded Quiet Parks International to protect it.

Emmy-winning (PBS NOVA) · 2x CASMA Winner · Grammy Voting Member · Quiet Parks International
